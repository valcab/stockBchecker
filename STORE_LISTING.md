# B-Stock Beacon Store Copy

This file contains draft presentation text for the Chrome Web Store in English and French.

## English

### Name

`B-Stock Beacon`

### Short Description

Track Thomann products and get alerted when B-Stock becomes available.

### Promotional Summary

B-Stock Beacon helps musicians watch Thomann product pages, detect B-Stock availability quickly, and keep track of price changes without manually refreshing product pages all day.

### Why This Extension Exists

Finding a good B-Stock deal on Thomann often comes down to timing. Popular products can appear and disappear quickly, and checking pages manually is repetitive and inefficient.  
B-Stock Beacon was created to make that process simple: track the products you care about, check them automatically, and get notified when a B-Stock offer appears.

### What It Does

- Track Thomann items from the extension popup or directly from product pages
- Detect when a B-Stock version becomes available
- Check all tracked items in one click
- Run automatic background checks at a chosen interval
- Show current availability, last check time, and price information
- Open the B-Stock page directly when it exists
- Filter, sort, and manage tracked items from a compact popup UI

### Key Benefits

- Save time by avoiding repeated manual checks
- React faster when B-Stock becomes available
- Keep your watchlist organized in one place
- Monitor multiple products at once
- Stay focused on music gear shopping, not page refreshing

### Detailed Description

B-Stock Beacon is a Chrome extension built for musicians, producers, and gear enthusiasts who regularly browse Thomann and want a faster way to watch B-Stock availability.

Instead of reopening the same product pages again and again, you can add products to a tracked list and let the extension do the checking for you. The extension monitors availability, displays product status clearly, keeps price information visible, and helps you jump directly to the B-Stock listing when it appears.

You can also use the floating action button on Thomann product pages to add or remove an item instantly from your tracked list.

Main features:

- Quick add from Thomann product pages
- Manual and automatic stock checks
- Desktop notifications when B-Stock is detected
- Availability badge on the extension icon
- Price and B-Stock price display
- Search, filtering, and sorting in the results view
- Import and export of tracked items
- Multilingual interface
- Light and dark theme support

This extension is designed to stay lightweight, fast, and focused on one job: helping you spot Thomann B-Stock opportunities more efficiently.

### Privacy-Oriented Notes

- No account required
- No external dashboard required
- Data is stored locally in the browser
- The extension is focused on Thomann tracking only
